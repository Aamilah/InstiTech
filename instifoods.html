InstiFoods

Instifoods is a H.I.T introductory spin-off company that trades under InstiTech Holdings. Established in 2009

the company produces various products, with current focus being a range of fruit pulp based cordials (concentrates and ready-to-drink), purified still water and dried fruits (banana and apple). The products and innovations produced within the spin-off company are research and innovation outputs from the Food Processing Technology Department of the H.I.T. To date the start-up boasts an automated production line, positioning the spin-off company for market penetration
